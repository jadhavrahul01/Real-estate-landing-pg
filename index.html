EXPERIENCE THE LUXURY

M3M, the leading real estate developer, has announced its latest commercial and residential project in Noida sector 94. The project is spread over 17.5 acres of land and offers world-class amenities and facilities. The residential project comprises luxurious apartments ranging from 3, 4 & 5 BHK. The apartments are designed with contemporary architecture and offer stunning views of the city. The commercial project comprises high-street retail shops, multiplexes, food courts, and office spaces. The project is designed to offer a seamless experience to its customers with ample parking spaces, 24/7 security, and power backup.
